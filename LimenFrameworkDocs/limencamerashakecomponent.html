ULimenCameraShakeComponent

A custom camera shake component that adjusts the intensity of camera shakes based on the speed of a pawn.

Overview

ULimenCameraShakeComponent is a custom camera shake component designed to dynamically adjust the intensity of camera shakes based on the movement speed of a pawn. It inherits from UCameraShakeSourceComponent and integrates with Unreal Engine's camera shake system to provide smooth and responsive visual effects during gameplay.

Usage & Implementation Notes

  • The component requires a UPawnMovementComponent to function correctly.

  • Camera shakes are managed through Unreal Engine's PlayerCameraManager.

  • The intensity of camera shakes is dynamically adjusted based on the pawn's movement speed, ensuring smooth and responsive visual effects during gameplay.
