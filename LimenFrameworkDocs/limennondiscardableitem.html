ALimenNonDiscardableItem

A subclass of ALimenNonPhysicalItem representing an item that cannot be discarded.

Overview

ALimenNonDiscardableItem is a Unreal Engine class designed to represent items within the Limen framework that are non-physical and cannot be discarded. It inherits from ALimenNonPhysicalItem.
