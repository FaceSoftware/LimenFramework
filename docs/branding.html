Branding and Assets

We love to see what you build with Limen Framework. To help you stay compliant with our license and to keep our community branding consistent, please follow these guidelines when attributing the framework in your project.

Logo Usage

You are encouraged to include the Limen Framework logo in your game’s 'Credits' or 'About' menu. Please use the official assets provided below.

Visual Identity

  • Primary Logo: Use the version with the full text "Limen Framework" for credit screens.

  • Icon: The standalone bracket icon may be used as a small badge or an entry in a "Powered By" list.

  • Clearance: Please maintain a minimum "safe zone" around the logo equal to 20% of its height to ensure readability.

Attribution Requirement

As per our license, if your application incorporates Limen Framework, you must include the following notice in your project's 'Credits' or 'About' section:

Powered by Limen Framework © FaceSoftware.
