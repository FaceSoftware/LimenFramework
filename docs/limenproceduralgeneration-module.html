FLimenProceduralGenerationModule

Manages the initialization and shutdown of the Limen Procedural Generation plugin.

Design Philosophy

The LimenProceduralGeneration module is designed to provide a robust framework for procedural generation within applications, ensuring efficient management and control over the lifecycle of the plugin.

Recommended Integration

  • Developers can use this system to integrate procedural generation capabilities into their applications.

  • It supports easy expansion and addition of new procedural generation algorithms or features.
