Licensing & Legal Requirements

Limen Framework is provided as a Source-Available project. We want to empower developers to create amazing games, but we must protect the framework's integrity.

License

Limen Framework is licensed under the PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0.

What you CAN do:

  • View, modify, and integrate the code into your own Unreal Engine projects.

  • Ship and sell games/applications built using the framework.

What you CANNOT do:

  • Sell, sublicense, or redistribute the framework as a standalone plugin or library.

  • Remove copyright notices.

Attribution

To comply with our license, you must include the following notice in your project's 'Credits' or 'About' section:

Powered by Limen Framework © FaceSoftware.

FMOD Integration

Limen Framework integrates with FMOD Studio. However, we do not bundle FMOD proprietary binaries.

Requirements:

  1. Valid License: You must hold a valid license with Firelight Technologies to use FMOD in your project.

  2. Binaries: You must manually install the FMOD SDK binaries into your Unreal Engine project.

  3. FMOD Attribution: In addition to crediting Limen Framework, you must follow FMOD's official attribution requirements in your game's credits.
